Some people call it a bucket list, list of life goals, or list of things to do before you die. Love This One Life will help you create, track, and share your list so you can start living the life you truly love.

There is no limit to the number of things you can put on your list and if you run out of ideas, we can offer suggestions of the most popular goals from other users that opt to share.

We offer you the option to share your ideas with others through your social networks or the LTOL community at large ... or you can keep your list all to yourself.

Features:

  • Create and track your list of life goals
  • Find suggestions for things to add to your list
  • Cross goals off your list as they are completed
  • Keep track of dates when goals were added and completed
  • Create plans to accomplish your goals
  • Share how you accomplished your goal when it is complete
  • Control what others can view from your list
  • Find others that share similar goals
  • Share updates via Facebook and Twitter
  • Add more goals to your list as you cross things off
